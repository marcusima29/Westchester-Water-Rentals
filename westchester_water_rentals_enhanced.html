Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

What's included in the rental?
Complete service including water slide, blower, professional delivery, setup, 8-hour rental period, and full breakdown. Every unit is sanitized before and after use.
How do I make a reservation?
Simply contact us with your preferred date and location. We'll handle all the details and confirm your booking within hours.
What if weather doesn't cooperate?
We offer free rescheduling or full refunds for weather-related cancellations. Safety and customer satisfaction are our top priorities.
Do you carry insurance?
Yes, we maintain comprehensive liability insurance for complete peace of mind. All equipment is regularly inspected and safety-certified.
What are your service areas?
We primarily serve Westchester, CA and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm service availability for your location.
