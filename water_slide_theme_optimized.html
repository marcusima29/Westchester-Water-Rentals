Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our water slide rentals

What's included in the rental?

Every rental includes delivery, professional setup, the water slide for 8 hours, and pickup/breakdown. We also provide basic safety instructions and are fully insured.

How much space do I need?

The HeroKiddo requires a 27'L x 9'W setup area, plus additional space for safe entry and exit. The area must be relatively flat and clear of sharp objects, trees, or overhead obstacles.

Do you provide water?

You'll need access to a standard garden hose and water source. We bring all the necessary hoses and connections to set up the water system.

What if it rains?

Safety first! We'll work with you to reschedule if weather conditions aren't suitable. We monitor weather forecasts and will contact you in advance if needed.
